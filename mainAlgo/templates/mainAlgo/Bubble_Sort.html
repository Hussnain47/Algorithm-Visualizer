Bubble Sort

Bubble sort, also known as sinking sort, is a basic sorting algorithm that iterates through a list, comparing nearby entries and swapping them if they are out of order. The list is sent through again and again until it is sorted.
The comparison sort method is called from the manner that smaller or bigger entries "bubble" to the top of the list.
