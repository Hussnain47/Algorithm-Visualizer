Quick Sort

Quicksort is based on the divide-and-conquer strategy. It operates by picking a 'pivot' element from the array and splitting the other items into two sub-arrays based on whether they are bigger or less than the pivot. As a result, it's also known as partition-exchange sort. The sub-arrays are then recursively sorted.
